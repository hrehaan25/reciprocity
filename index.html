The submission is sent to the student's designated School Administrator (Moderator) who must review the skills for appropriateness and confirm the student's eligibility to participate based on the school's internal policies.

Note: No contact information is exchanged during this stage. The Moderator only sees the content and the student's verified, anonymized school ID.

Secure Matching & Exchange ü§ù

Once approved, the system uses the Core Matchmaking Algorithm (CMA) to find a high-utility, reciprocal match with another vetted student from an approved institution.

Communication occurs through our **Secure Communication Relay (SCR)**, which logs, audits, and filters all dialogue in real-time to prevent the exchange of Private Personal Information (PPI), harassment, or off-topic content.

Upon completion, both students provide anonymous feedback on the exchange's success and professionalism. This data is used to build a robust trust rating for each profile, managed by their respective Moderators.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is student privacy protected?

Student privacy is our top priority. Reciprocity is designed with several layers of protection:

  • **Anonymized Profiles:** Initial skill submission and matching is based only on skill, institution, and anonymous ID.
  • **Zero-Knowledge Logging:** The Secure Communication Relay (SCR) audits all exchanges in real-time but stores messages in an encrypted, zero-knowledge format that is not accessible to Reciprocity staff unless legally required.
  • **No PII Exchange:** The system blocks and flags attempts to share private, unverified contact information.
What happens if a student violates the Code of Conduct?

Violations are managed by the student's home institution. Reciprocity maintains a strict, zero-tolerance policy:

  1. Any attempt to solicit or share personal, unverified contact information (mobile numbers, private social media links) is an immediate, reportable offense.
  2. Off-topic, harassing, or sexually explicit content results in immediate permanent banishment from the platform, notified to the Moderator.

The Moderator Activity Dashboard (MAD) gives admins all the necessary tools to track, intervene, and manage sanctions.

Can students exchange with international partners?

Yes, but this feature is now included in **all tiers** (removed cross icon) and is specifically designed for institutions subscribed to the **Global Tier** where the International Compliance Toolkit (ICT) is included.

The Global Tier includes the International Compliance Toolkit (ICT) to handle multi-jurisdictional data compliance (GDPR, COPPA, etc.) and allows for unlimited exchange radius. Institutional Plan users are limited to regional exchanges (100km radius).

Review & Safety Consent

By proceeding, you acknowledge and consent to the following mandatory safety protocols:

1. Administrator Vetting & Approval (Mandatory)

Your request will be reviewed and approved by your school's designated Administrator/Moderator before any matching can occur. Your Moderator has full access to the content of your submission.

2. Secure Communication Relay (SCR)

All communication related to the exchange will be logged, audited, and filtered by the system. Any attempt to solicit or share private PII outside of the platform will result in immediate termination of the exchange and notification to your Moderator.

3. Location Privacy

Reciprocity respects your privacy. Matchmaking is based on regional proximity, not exact location. No personal address or precise GPS data is ever stored or shared.
